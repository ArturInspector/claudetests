# Topic: English Grammar and Speaking (B1 Level)

## Concept: Present Perfect vs Past Simple
**Tags**: grammar, tenses, present-perfect, past-simple, speaking
**Estimated Time**: 20 minutes

### Level 1: Когда использовать Present Perfect?
Объясни разницу между "I did" и "I have done" с примерами.

**Answer**:
**Present Perfect** используется для действий, которые:
- Завершились в прошлом, но результат важен сейчас
- Произошли в неопределенное время (без конкретной даты)
- Связаны с настоящим моментом

**Past Simple** используется для действий:
- В определенное время в прошлом
- Завершенных и не связанных с настоящим

**Примеры**:
```
Present Perfect:
- I have lost my keys. (Не знаю где они сейчас)
- She has been to Paris. (Опыт в жизни)
- Have you finished your homework? (Важно сейчас)

Past Simple:
- I lost my keys yesterday. (Конкретное время)
- She went to Paris in 2019. (Конкретная дата)
- Did you finish your homework? (Просто факт)
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "I've never tried sushi before." (опыт)
- "I tried sushi last week." (конкретное время)
- "Have you seen this movie?" (все еще актуально)
- "Did you see the movie yesterday?" (конкретное время)

### Level 2: Present Perfect Continuous
Объясни когда использовать "I have been doing" вместо "I have done".

**Answer**:
**Present Perfect Continuous** подчеркивает:
- Длительность действия (how long)
- Действие все еще продолжается
- Временной период

**Present Perfect Simple** подчеркивает:
- Результат действия
- Завершенность
- Сколько раз (how many times)

**Примеры**:
```
Continuous (длительность):
- I have been studying English for 3 years. (все еще учу)
- She has been working here since 2020. (все еще работает)
- How long have you been waiting? (с какого времени)

Simple (результат):
- I have studied English. (знаю английский - результат)
- She has worked here. (опыт работы)
- How many times have you been there? (количество)
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "I've been thinking about it." (процесс мышления)
- "I've thought about it." (принял решение)
- "What have you been doing?" (чем занимался недавно)
- "What have you done?" (что сделал, результат)

### Level 3: Past Perfect и Past Perfect Continuous
Объясни использование "had done" и "had been doing" в рассказах.

**Answer**:
**Past Perfect** показывает действие, которое произошло ДО другого действия в прошлом.

**Структура**: Past Perfect → Past Simple
```
- When I arrived, the train had already left.
- She had finished dinner before I called.
- They had never seen such a beautiful sunset.
```

**Past Perfect Continuous** показывает длительное действие до момента в прошлом:
```
- I had been waiting for 2 hours when she finally arrived.
- He had been studying all night before the exam.
- How long had you been living there before you moved?
```

**Разговорные фразы в рассказах**:
- "I had just finished when..." (только что закончил)
- "By the time I got there, they had already left." (к тому времени)
- "I had been working there for 5 years when I quit." (работал до момента)

### Level 4: Сложные случаи и исключения
Объясни нюансы с "for/since", "already/yet/just", и статические глаголы.

**Answer**:
**For vs Since**:
- **For** + период времени: "for 3 years", "for a week"
- **Since** + точка времени: "since 2020", "since Monday"

```
- I've lived here for 5 years. (сколько времени)
- I've lived here since 2019. (с какого времени)
- I've been waiting for 2 hours.
- I've been waiting since 3 o'clock.
```

**Already/Yet/Just**:
- **Already** - уже (в утверждениях): "I've already done it."
- **Yet** - еще (в вопросах/отрицаниях): "Haven't you finished yet?"
- **Just** - только что: "I've just arrived."

**Статические глаголы** (не используют Continuous):
- know, understand, believe, like, love, hate, want, need
- "I've known him for years." (НЕ "I've been knowing")
- "I've wanted this for a long time." (НЕ "I've been wanting")

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "Have you finished yet?" - "Not yet, I'm still working on it."
- "I've just realized something important."
- "I've already told you about it."

## Concept: Conditionals (Условные предложения)
**Tags**: grammar, conditionals, if-clauses, speaking, hypothetical
**Estimated Time**: 25 minutes

### Level 1: Zero, First, Second Conditional
Объясни три типа условных предложений с примерами.

**Answer**:
**Zero Conditional** - общие истины, факты:
```
If + Present Simple, Present Simple
- If you heat water to 100°C, it boils.
- If I don't sleep well, I feel tired.
- "If you press this button, the light turns on."
```

**First Conditional** - реальные будущие ситуации:
```
If + Present Simple, will + infinitive
- If it rains tomorrow, I will stay home.
- If you study hard, you will pass the exam.
- "If I see him, I'll tell him about it."
```

**Second Conditional** - нереальные/гипотетические ситуации:
```
If + Past Simple, would + infinitive
- If I won the lottery, I would travel the world.
- If I were you, I would accept the offer.
- "If I had more time, I would learn Spanish."
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "If I were you, I'd..." (совет)
- "What would you do if...?" (гипотетический вопрос)
- "If it were possible, I would..." (нереальная ситуация)

### Level 2: Third Conditional и Mixed Conditionals
Объясни "If I had done" и смешанные типы.

**Answer**:
**Third Conditional** - о прошлом, которое нельзя изменить:
```
If + Past Perfect, would have + past participle
- If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam.
- If she had called me, I would have helped her.
- "If I had known, I would have come earlier."
```

**Mixed Conditionals** - комбинация времен:
```
Past condition → Present result:
If + Past Perfect, would + infinitive
- If I had studied medicine, I would be a doctor now.
- If she had accepted the job, she would live in London.

Present condition → Past result:
If + Past Simple, would have + past participle
- If I were better at math, I would have become an engineer.
- If she liked traveling, she would have visited more countries.
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "If only I had..." (сожаление о прошлом)
- "I wish I had..." (желание изменить прошлое)
- "If I had been there, I would have..." (альтернативное прошлое)

### Level 3: Unless, As long as, Provided that
Объясни альтернативные способы выражения условий.

**Answer**:
**Unless** = if not:
```
- I won't go unless you come with me. (= if you don't come)
- Unless it rains, we'll have a picnic.
- "I can't help you unless you tell me the truth."
```

**As long as / So long as** = при условии что:
```
- You can borrow my car as long as you drive carefully.
- I'll help you as long as you promise to study.
- "As long as you're happy, I'm happy."
```

**Provided that / Providing that** = при условии:
```
- You can enter provided that you have a ticket.
- I'll agree providing that you pay in advance.
```

**Even if** = даже если:
```
- I'll go even if it rains. (в любом случае пойду)
- Even if you apologize, I won't forgive you.
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "As long as you're here, everything is fine."
- "Unless something changes, we'll have problems."
- "Even if it's difficult, I'll try."

### Level 4: I wish и If only
Объясни выражения желаний и сожалений.

**Answer**:
**I wish + Past Simple** - желание изменить настоящее:
```
- I wish I had more time. (сейчас нет времени)
- I wish I knew the answer. (не знаю ответ)
- "I wish I were taller." (не высокий)
```

**I wish + Past Perfect** - сожаление о прошлом:
```
- I wish I had studied harder. (не учился усердно)
- I wish I hadn't said that. (сказал, жалею)
- "I wish I had listened to your advice."
```

**If only** - более эмоционально:
```
- If only I had more money! (очень хочу)
- If only I had known earlier! (сильное сожаление)
- "If only I could speak French!"
```

**I wish + would** - желание изменить поведение других:
```
- I wish you would stop talking. (хочу чтобы ты перестал)
- I wish it would stop raining. (о погоде)
- "I wish he would call me."
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "I wish I could..." (желание способности)
- "If only I had..." (сильное сожаление)
- "I wish you would..." (просьба/жалоба)

**Resources**:
- [Article] https://learnenglish.britishcouncil.org/grammar/b1-b2-grammar/conditionals - British Council Conditionals
- [Video] https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4H3-2sm1lTs - All Conditionals объяснение

## Concept: Passive Voice (Страдательный залог)
**Tags**: grammar, passive-voice, sentence-structure, formal-english
**Estimated Time**: 15 minutes

### Level 1: Когда использовать Passive Voice?
Объясни структуру и случаи использования.

**Answer**:
**Passive Voice** используется когда:
- Объект действия важнее субъекта
- Субъект неизвестен или неважен
- В формальном/научном стиле

**Структура**: be + past participle
```
Active: Someone stole my bike.
Passive: My bike was stolen.

Active: They built this house in 1990.
Passive: This house was built in 1990.
```

**Разные времена**:
```
Present Simple: The car is washed every week.
Past Simple: The car was washed yesterday.
Present Perfect: The car has been washed.
Future: The car will be washed tomorrow.
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "I was told that..." (мне сказали)
- "It's said that..." (говорят что)
- "The meeting was cancelled." (вместо "They cancelled")

### Level 2: Passive с модальными глаголами
Объясни "must be done", "should have been done".

**Answer**:
**Модальные + be + past participle**:
```
- The work must be finished by Friday.
- This problem should be solved immediately.
- The door can be opened with this key.
- "This needs to be done today."
```

**Perfect Passive**:
```
- The work should have been finished yesterday.
- The problem could have been solved earlier.
- "It must have been difficult for you."
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "It should be done by now."
- "This needs to be fixed."
- "It might have been stolen."

### Level 3: Passive с двумя объектами
Объясни "I was given" vs "It was given to me".

**Answer**:
Когда глагол имеет два объекта, возможны два варианта:
```
Active: They gave me a book.
Passive 1: I was given a book. (личный объект становится подлежащим)
Passive 2: A book was given to me. (предмет становится подлежащим)
```

**Другие примеры**:
```
- I was told the news. / The news was told to me.
- She was shown the way. / The way was shown to her.
- "We were offered a job." / "A job was offered to us."
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "I was asked to..." (меня попросили)
- "We were told that..." (нам сказали)
- "She was given a chance." (ей дали шанс)

**Resources**:
- [Article] https://learnenglish.britishcouncil.org/grammar/b1-b2-grammar/passive-voice - British Council Passive

## Concept: Reported Speech (Косвенная речь)
**Tags**: grammar, reported-speech, indirect-speech, speaking
**Estimated Time**: 20 minutes

### Level 1: Основные изменения времен
Объясни как меняются времена в косвенной речи.

**Answer**:
При переводе прямой речи в косвенную времена сдвигаются назад:

```
Direct: "I am tired." → Reported: He said (that) he was tired.
Direct: "I work here." → Reported: He said (that) he worked there.
Direct: "I will come." → Reported: He said (that) he would come.
Direct: "I have finished." → Reported: He said (that) he had finished.
```

**Таблица изменений**:
```
Present Simple → Past Simple
Present Continuous → Past Continuous
Past Simple → Past Perfect
Will → Would
Can → Could
Must → Had to
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "He said he was busy."
- "She told me she would come."
- "They said they had finished."

### Level 2: Say vs Tell, Ask vs Request
Объясни разницу в использовании.

**Answer**:
**Say** - без объекта: "He said (that) he was tired."
**Tell** - с объектом: "He told me (that) he was tired."

```
- She said she was leaving. (просто сказала)
- She told me she was leaving. (сказала МНЕ)
- "I told you so!" (я же говорил)
```

**Ask** - для вопросов:
```
- He asked if I was ready.
- She asked me where I lived.
- "I asked him what time it was."
```

**Request** - более формально:
```
- He requested that I come early.
- "They requested permission to leave."
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "I told you..." (я тебе говорил)
- "She asked me to..." (она попросила меня)
- "He said that..." (он сказал что)

### Level 3: Вопросы и команды в косвенной речи
Объясни как передавать вопросы и приказы.

**Answer**:
**Вопросы** используют "if/whether" или порядок слов меняется:
```
Direct: "Are you ready?" → Reported: He asked if I was ready.
Direct: "Where do you live?" → Reported: He asked where I lived.
Direct: "What time is it?" → Reported: She asked what time it was.
```

**Команды и просьбы** используют "to + infinitive":
```
Direct: "Close the door." → Reported: He told me to close the door.
Direct: "Please help me." → Reported: She asked me to help her.
Direct: "Don't smoke here." → Reported: They told me not to smoke there.
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "He asked me to help."
- "She told me not to worry."
- "I asked if he was coming."

**Resources**:
- [Article] https://learnenglish.britishcouncil.org/grammar/b1-b2-grammar/reported-speech - British Council Reported Speech

**Related Concepts**: Direct Speech, Questions, Commands, Verb Patterns

## Concept: Phrasal Verbs (Фразовые глаголы)
**Tags**: vocabulary, phrasal-verbs, speaking, idioms
**Estimated Time**: 25 minutes

### Level 1: Основные фразовые глаголы
Объясни структуру и самые частые фразовые глаголы.

**Answer**:
**Phrasal Verbs** = глагол + предлог/наречие

**Разделяемые** (можно вставить объект между):
```
- Turn on: "Turn on the light" / "Turn the light on"
- Put off: "Put off the meeting" / "Put the meeting off"
- Give up: "Give up smoking" / "Give it up"
```

**Неразделяемые**:
```
- Look after: "Look after the children" (НЕ "look the children after")
- Get on with: "Get on with your work"
- Run into: "I ran into an old friend"
```

**Топ-10 для B1**:
```
1. Look for - искать
2. Find out - выяснить
3. Give up - сдаться, бросить
4. Put off - отложить
5. Turn on/off - включить/выключить
6. Get up - вставать
7. Look after - присматривать
8. Come across - наткнуться
9. Break down - сломаться
10. Work out - решить, тренироваться
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "I'm looking for my keys."
- "Can you find out what time it starts?"
- "Don't give up!"

### Level 2: Фразовые глаголы с несколькими значениями
Объясни контекстные значения.

**Answer**:
Многие фразовые глаголы имеют несколько значений:

**Get**:
```
- Get up - вставать: "I get up at 7."
- Get on - ладить: "We get on well."
- Get over - оправиться: "I can't get over it."
- Get along - ладить: "Do you get along with your boss?"
```

**Take**:
```
- Take off - взлететь, снять: "The plane took off." / "Take off your coat."
- Take up - начать заниматься: "I took up yoga."
- Take after - быть похожим: "She takes after her mother."
```

**Put**:
```
- Put on - надеть: "Put on your shoes."
- Put off - отложить: "Let's put it off until tomorrow."
- Put up with - терпеть: "I can't put up with this noise."
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "How are you getting on?" (как дела?)
- "I need to get over this." (пережить)
- "What are you taking up?" (чем занимаешься?)

### Level 3: Формальные эквиваленты
Объясни когда использовать формальные синонимы.

**Answer**:
В формальной речи фразовые глаголы часто заменяются одним словом:

```
Informal → Formal:
- Put off → Postpone: "Let's postpone the meeting."
- Find out → Discover: "We need to discover the truth."
- Give up → Quit/Abandon: "Don't quit now."
- Look into → Investigate: "We'll investigate the matter."
- Get back → Return: "When will you return?"
```

**Разговорные vs формальные**:
```
- "I'll look into it." (разговорное)
- "I'll investigate it." (формальное)

- "Can you put me up?" (разговорное - разместить)
- "Can you accommodate me?" (формальное)
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "Let's put it off" (informal)
- "Let's postpone it" (formal)
- "I'll look into that" (informal)
- "I'll investigate that" (formal)

## Concept: Useful Speaking Phrases (Полезные разговорные фразы)
**Tags**: speaking, phrases, conversation, expressions, b1-level
**Estimated Time**: 15 minutes

### Level 1: Согласие и несогласие
Объясни как выражать мнение вежливо.

**Answer**:
**Согласие**:
```
- I agree with you.
- That's a good point.
- You're absolutely right.
- I couldn't agree more.
- "That makes sense."
```

**Несогласие** (вежливо):
```
- I see what you mean, but...
- I'm not sure I agree.
- That's true, however...
- I have a different opinion.
- "I'm afraid I disagree."
```

**Частичное согласие**:
```
- I partly agree.
- That's true to some extent.
- I agree with you up to a point.
- "You have a point, but..."
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "I totally agree!"
- "I'm not so sure about that."
- "Fair enough, but..."

### Level 2: Выражение мнения
Объясни как делиться мыслями.

**Answer**:
**Начало высказывания**:
```
- In my opinion...
- I think/believe that...
- From my point of view...
- As far as I'm concerned...
- "If you ask me..."
```

**Подкрепление мнения**:
```
- The way I see it...
- It seems to me that...
- I have the feeling that...
- "To be honest..."
```

**Изменение темы**:
```
- By the way...
- Speaking of...
- That reminds me...
- "On another note..."
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "In my view, this is important."
- "To be honest, I don't think so."
- "By the way, did you hear about...?"

### Level 3: Просьбы и предложения
Объясни вежливые способы просить и предлагать.

**Answer**:
**Просьбы** (от менее к более формальным):
```
- Can you...?
- Could you...? (вежливее)
- Would you mind...?
- I wonder if you could...
- "Would it be possible to...?"
```

**Предложения**:
```
- How about...?
- What about...?
- Why don't we...?
- Let's...
- "I suggest we..."
```

**Ответы на предложения**:
```
- That sounds great!
- Good idea!
- I'm not sure about that.
- I'd rather not.
- "That works for me."
```

**Разговорные фразы**:
- "Could you possibly...?"
- "How about we meet at 5?"
- "That sounds good to me!"
